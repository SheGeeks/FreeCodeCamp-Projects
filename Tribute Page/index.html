Lisa "Left Eye" Lopes

May 27, 1971. Lisa Nicole Lopes was born and raised in Philadelphia, PA. Lopes formed the musical trio The Lopes Kids with her siblings, with whom she sang gospel songs at local events and churches. In 1990, Lopes moved to Atlanta to audition at an opening casting call for an new girl group, that would become TLC. Lopes took a spiritual retreat to Honduras to and had been in the process of setting up two educational centers for Honduran children. At the tender age of 30, Lopes died in a fatal car crash in Honduras.

Rozonda "Chilli" Thomas

February 27, 1971. Born in Columbus, Georgia, Thomas was first a dancer for Damian Dame. In 1991, she joined TLC, replacing founding member Crystal Jones, and was nicknamed "Chilli" by Lisa Lopes to retain the group's name, TLC. In 2012, Thomas started a non-profit organization called Chilli's Crew. The organization is an Atlanta program targeted to help girls between the ages of 13-17 with self-esteem issues.
